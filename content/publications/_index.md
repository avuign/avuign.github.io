## Research Papers

### Einstein gravity from a matrix integral — Part II
**S. Komatsu, A. Martina, J. Penedones, A. Vuignier, X. Zhao**  
arXiv:2411.18678 (2024)

Using supersymmetric localization, we compute the partition function and protected correlators of the polarized IKKT matrix model. We find that the original IKKT model differs from polarized IKKT in the limit of vanishing mass deformation, and recover the electrostatic problem defining the gravity dual.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.18678)

### Einstein gravity from a matrix integral — Part I
**S. Komatsu, A. Martina, J. Penedones, A. Vuignier, X. Zhao**  
arXiv:2410.18173 (2024)

We construct backreacted geometries dual to the supersymmetric mass deformation of the IKKT matrix model. These are Euclidean type IIB supergravity solutions with SO(7)×SO(3) isometry and 16 supersymmetries. Quantizing fluxes, we establish a one-to-one correspondence between supergravity solutions and fuzzy sphere vacua.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.18173)

### Gravity from quantum mechanics of finite matrices
**S. Komatsu, A. Martina, J. Penedones, N. Suchel, A. Vuignier, X. Zhao**  
arXiv:2401.16471 (2024)

We revisit the BMN conjecture relating M-theory on a PP-wave background and Matrix Quantum Mechanics. At strong coupling and finite N, we derive an effective Hamiltonian describing non-relativistic free particles in a harmonic trap, matching the supergravity excitation spectrum.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.16471)

### The O(N) Monolith reloaded: Sum rules and Form Factor Bootstrap
**L. Córdova, A. Georgoudis, J. Penedones, A. Vuignier**  
arXiv:2311.03031 (2023)

We revisit the O(N) nonlinear sigma model using the S-matrix bootstrap combined with form factor sum rules. We derive constraints on scattering amplitudes and form factors, extending previous results on the "O(N) monolith" — the allowed region in coupling space.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.03031)

### Injecting the UV into the bootstrap: Ising Field Theory
**M. Correia, J. Penedones, A. Vuignier**  
JHEP 08 (2023) 108 · arXiv:2212.03917

We develop a dual S-matrix bootstrap setup in 2D incorporating scattering amplitudes and matrix elements of local operators. Using the stress tensor, we input UV constraints on IR observables via the central charge c_UV. We establish rigorous bounds on c_UV for ℤ₂-symmetric scalar theories and target Ising Field Theory.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.03917) · [JHEP](https://doi.org/10.1007/JHEP08(2023)108)
