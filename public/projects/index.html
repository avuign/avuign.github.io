Building an LLM from Scratch#

My first exercise in machine learning was to teach my computer to recognize handwritten digits.

Then, to learn LLMs I started by implementing a simple character-generation model

The next step is to have real text generation, and for that I’m working through Sebastian Raschka’s book Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch) to learn how to implement a GPT-style model step by step in PyTorch. The goal is to understand transformers at a low level: tokenization, attention, training loops, and text generation. My current repo (in progress)
